What is Lunch Roulette all about?

What is Lunch Roulette?

A lunch match-making program! If you sign up for Lunch Roulette, you'll be matched with a random co-worker once every month. You'll both get an email notifying you of your lunch partner. Then it's up to you to pick the place.

Can I join? I'm just a...

Yes, you can join! Lunch Roulette is all about meeting people outside your circles - everybody is welcome to sign up, from interns to Ps.

Do I have to go to lunch if I sign up?

We hope you do! Lunch Roulette only works if you follow through with your lunch commitment. If you don't feel like lunch after you sign up, you can pause or cancel your participation at any time.

Why does Lunch Roulette exist?

Building diverse networks helps us all! The RBNA Trainee Diversity & Inclusion Committee, along with RBNA/HRC1.3, sponsor the Lunch Roulette program as a way to help associates meet each other and learn about the organization.
